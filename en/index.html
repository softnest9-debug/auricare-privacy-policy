1. General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how the AuriCare – Hearing Aid App (the “App”) processes data. We respect user privacy and limit processing strictly to what is required for the App to function.

The App operates locally on the device – audio is processed directly on the phone and is not transmitted or stored on any external servers.

2. Scope and Data Processing

  • The App does not collect, profile, or permanently store personal data.
  • Captured audio is used only for amplification and noise reduction and is processed locally.
  • No audio is recorded or transmitted outside the user’s device.
  • The App does not access location data or information from other apps.

3. App Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • Microphone (audio recording) – to capture and process ambient sound locally on the device.
  • Nearby devices / Bluetooth – to support headphones and audio accessories.
  • Notifications – to inform the user that the App is running in the background.
  • Background operation – to ensure uninterrupted functionality.

Denying these permissions may limit or prevent the App from functioning properly.

4. Trial Period and Payments

The App offers a 7‑day free trial. After the trial, users may choose a subscription or a one‑time purchase to continue using the full functionality.

Payments are processed by Google Play and are subject to Google’s Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.
